iPad From Metro

03 Sep 2010

Unbelievable and yet, true – they are selling iPads in Metro! If you don’t believe it, check their online catalogue (you need a Metro card to be able to do so).

The price is not that impressive (keeping in mind that the same model of iPad sells for $499 in the US), but it is better than what others are offering in Bulgaria. What impresses me most is that there is an Apple product on sale in a wholesale store like Metro.

If you like the offer, hurry up – it expires on Sep 8th. I suspect that the price will be higher after this date.

In this context, Apple announced the new Apple TV the other day. You can see the recording of the keynote here. Even if you don’t own any Apple product (like myself), Steve Jobs’ presentations are always worth watching.
